Introduction

Star Wars The Clone Wars, created by George Lucas is an animated TV show set in the Star Wars Universe between the Films, Attack of the Clones and Revenge of the Sith and details the events of The Clone Wars a plot point glossed over in those two prequel films. The show is seen by many to be the definitive part of the Star Wars Prequel Era, essentially fixing the issues that the prequel films created and has since gained a Cult following from its introduction in 2008 and Introduced forever Popular Characters such as Ahsoka Tano, Asajj Ventress and Cad Bane.

After Airing 5 Seasons and not long after the Disney Acquisition of Lucasfilm In early 2013, it was announced that the Clone Wars would be cancelled and further production on episodes would cease as production switched to Star Wars Rebels, despite this many plans had already been set in place to conclude the show with another 65 Episodes planned to be released, while 13 of these which were already completed at the time of the cancelation and where later released in March 2014, the other 52 consisting of 13 arcs where left unfinished, while 3 of the Arcs where released in other media, that only accounts for 12 episodes leaving another 40 episodes without any official release in any capacity.

In July 2018 it was announced that the Clone Wars would be revived for the upcoming Disney+ service with 12 new episodes to debut on Disney+, It was further announced in April 2019 that these 12 episodes would consist of 3 Unreleased Arcs being: The Bad Batch, Ahsokas Journey and The Siege of Mandalore.

What are the 13 Unfinished Stories?

Bounty Hunter Cad Bane and Boba Fett team up for a Rescue Mission on Tatooine to rescue a child. This would have been the last main Bounty Hunter story and feature pretty much all Bounty Hunters that have been on the show including Aurra Sing and Bossk. This story would have featured a duel between Boba Fett and Cad Bane, resulting in the formers bullet mark on his helmet and the latters supposed death. Also this story is rumoured to contain the Death of Aurra Sing.

Crystal Crisis on Utapau Obi-Wan and Anakin Investigate the death of a Jedi on Utapau and discover that the Separatists are trying to get a hold of Giant Kybercrystals to use for a mysterious purpose. This story was released in its Proxy Animated format with final voice acting on the Lost Missions Blu-Ray and The Clone Wars Legacy (Which has now had all of its pages removed)

Ahsokas Walkabout Ahsoka, having left the Jedi Order is now trying to find her path in life, she ventures to level 1313 on Coruscant and meets a Scoundrel called Nyx. Due to the bias views of the Jedi in the lower levels of Coruscant, Ahsoka must hide her Jedi teachings while getting inadvertently involved with the Pike Syndicate.

The Bad Batch After many defeats by the Separatists on Anaxes, The Republic enlist the help of Clone Force 99 aka the Bad Batch, here they discover that Echo a Clone Thought to have been killed during the liberation at the Citadel, is alive and is being used by the Techno Union to predict the Republics strategies. Admiral Trench appears yet again throughout this Arc and is finally dealt with by Anakin. Like Crystal Crisis on Utapau, this arc was released in its Proxy Animated form again with Final Voice Acting on the Star Wars.com Clone Wars Legacy page.

Dark Disciple (Part 1) Quinlan Vos is tasked by the Jedi Council to team up with Recent Bounty Hunter Asajj Ventress to Assassinate Count Dooku, throughout the experience they form a romantic connection but fail in their task and Vos ends up captured, after seeing Vos seemly fall to the Dark Side, Ventress gives up trying to rescue him. Both parts of this story where combined into the Dark Disciple Novel

The Bad Batch on Kashyyyk Yoda along with Clone Force 99 go to Kashyyyk and help the Wookies against an Separatist attack aided by the Trandoshans. During this arc we were also going to get some development for the planet itself including the Wookies Relationship with the trees.

Son of Dathomir Captured by Sidious, Maul is tortured to find out the whereabouts of Mother Talzin, a threat to Sidious Empire. Once Maul escapes, Grievous and Dooku are tasked with getting him back. Dooku gets captured and is used by Mother Talzin to return to her Physical Form, once Physical again Mother Talzin is killed by Grievous and Maul is left to retreat and is deemed not a threat by Sidious. This arc was finalised into a Comic released under the title Darth Maul Son of Dathomir.

Return to the Jedi (Sith Shrine) Yoda goes missing and after Ahsoka discovers something dire in the lower depths of Coruscant, she enlists the help of Obi-Wan and Anakin to delve into the lower levels to rescue Yoda and discover the Sith Shrine, will also preventing Sidious from accessing the Holocron Vault.

Dark Disciple (Part 2) After some time passes Since Ventress failure to assassinate Dooku, Ventress decides to team up with the Jedi Order to Rescue Him, sensing something is not right with Quinlan, Ventress decides to part ways with him while the Jedi Order use him to gain an advantage in the war with the knowledge he has gained while captured. As the Jedi realise the information Quinlan has been giving them is incorrect, Ventress suspicions are confirmed and he has indeed fallen to the Dark Side, to save Quinlan and bring him back to the light, Ventress makes the ultimate sacrifice. Both parts of this story where combined into the Dark Disciple Novel

Rex and R2 Top Gun The Pilots and Droids of a Republic Cruiser get into an argument, this leads to Rex and R2 somehow getting stranded on one of Ryloths many moons, throughout the experience both learn about each others roles during the war such as why the clones hate droids and their mistreatment of them. They also reprogram a B1 Battledroid.

Yuuzhan Vong / Grysk An Yuuzhan Vong scout ship is trying to assess the strength of the Republic and the Jedi Order, like an X-Files episode this story would have had been an Alien Abduction story set in the Star Wars Universe and is assumed that they take an unknown Jedi with the Jedi Order trying to find out what happened.

Return to Mon Cala Palpatine has spent years and years as the Chancellor despite being overdue to be replaced. Padme tries to find a way to work against Palpatines prolonged stay in Office, later on she returns to Mon Cala and upon arrival discovers that the Quarren Leader has been murdered. Padme later finds out that the Mon Cala see the government as Gridlocked. It is also assumed that during this story Padme would have found out she was pregnant.

The Siege of Mandalore Bo-Katan enlists the help of Ahsoka who in turn contacts the Jedi Order, to displace Maul from Mandalore since his return after the death of Talzin, while Anakin lends Ahsoka a sub division of the 501st being the 332nd with Rex in toe, Obi-Wan and Anakin are called back to Coruscant to rescue the Chancellor. Ahsoka, Bo and Rex lead the charge to claim back Mandalore from Maul but end up getting Distracted due to Order 66 taking place forcing Ahsoka to abandon her conquest for Maul and go into hiding.

Predicated Creation Method of Episodes

With the previous Clone Wars production, it seems that George Lucas would give writers a general idea of a story, one writer would agree to write the story before collaborating with the other team members. The script would then be proxy animated while adding temp voices, before cutting scenes to fit a 22-minute runtime, this would make sure that they dont waste expenses on Voice Actors, Animation, GFX and SFX on scenes that wont be used. This would also make sense as to why the Deleted Scenes of episodes are in their proxy animated forms with their temp voice actors as they wouldnt have been developed any further.

After cutting down the Proxy Animation they would then incorporate the final voice work as shown in the released story reels for Crystal Crisis on Utapau and The Bad Batch into the episodes before incorporating the Animation, SFX and GFX layers which would presumably be the most expensive and take the most time to produce.

I also presume that multiple processes are going on at once such as once the episode has been cut down to 22 minutes, they would likely produce the voice acting at the same time as the GFX, and non-speech related animation due to them not relying on each other and more than likely consist of separate people working on it.

The soundtrack seems to be the last piece of work that is put into an episode considering a clip from Season 5 Episode 16 The Lawless was shown at Comic Con, July 2012 with everything finished apart from the soundtrack that instead used sample music from the Prequel and Original Trilogy OST. https://youtu.be/JUnmHmjL4eE
